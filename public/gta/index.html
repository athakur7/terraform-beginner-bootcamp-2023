How to Play GTA

Welcome to the world of Grand Theft Auto (GTA). GTA is an open-world action-adventure game series known for its immersive gameplay, exciting missions, and vibrant in-game world.

Here are some tips on how to get started:

  • Explore the open world: GTA games offer a vast open world with cities, countryside, and more. Explore it to discover hidden secrets and missions.
  • Complete missions: Follow the storyline and complete missions to progress in the game and unlock new content.
  • Interact with NPCs: Engage with non-playable characters for interesting interactions and side missions.
  • Customize your character: Personalize your character's appearance and skills to suit your playstyle.
