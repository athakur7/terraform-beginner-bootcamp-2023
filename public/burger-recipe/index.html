Classic Beef Burger

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cheese slice
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion, and condiments of your choice
  • Burger bun

Instructions:

  1. Season the beef patty with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook the patty on a grill or stovetop until it reaches your desired level of doneness.
  3. Place a slice of cheese on top of the patty while it's still hot.
  4. Assemble the burger with lettuce, tomato, onion, and your favorite condiments on a burger bun.
